How the Donation Process Works?

1. Student Applies for Course

Students select a course and apply for available financial aid opportunities.

2. Provide Required Documents

Students submit all necessary documents for verification and eligibility checks.

3. Donor Donates

Donors contribute to the education fund and make donations available to students.

4. Matchmaking Algorithm

Our algorithm matches students with donors based on their funding needs and course requirements.

5. Donors Receive Funding Confirmation

Donors are notified when their contributions are successfully allocated to students.

6. Completion

The funding process is complete, and students receive the necessary financial aid to pursue their education.
